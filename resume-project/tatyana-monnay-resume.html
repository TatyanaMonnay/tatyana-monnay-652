Tatyana Monnay

Multimedia Investigative Journalist

"The way to right wrongs is to turn the light of truth upon them." - Ida B. Wells

Thanks for stopping by!

I'm a multimedia journalist based in Washington, DC. Born and raised in South Florida, I grew up fascinated by the world of journalism. As the daughter of a journalist, I grew up in the newsroom. Since then, I've been building hard skills in research, audience engagement and multimedia producing and editing. I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Journalism at the University of Maryland and received my Bachelor's degree in Journalism with an emphasis in Multimedia Investigative Reporting from the Missouri School of Journalism. 

In the newsroom, I'm exploring my passion for multimedia storytelling and expanding the reach of stories through innovative audience engagement strategies.

When I'm not looking for trends in data and my eyes aren't blurry from doomscrolling on Twitter, I like to take time to disconnect and deepen my yoga practice.
